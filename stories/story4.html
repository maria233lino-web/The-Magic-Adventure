📖 While Reading

Lily was a kind girl who loved nature. Every afternoon, she walked through the forest near her village. One day, she found a small crystal on the ground. The crystal sparkled with many colors. Lily picked it up and suddenly a tiny fairy appeared. The fairy had shining wings and a bright smile. "Hello, Lily," said the fairy. "My name is Crystal." Lily was surprised. "A fairy?" she asked. Crystal nodded. "I need your help," she said. "The Magic Tree is losing its light." Lily and Crystal followed a path through the forest. They crossed a stream and climbed a small hill. At last, they reached the Magic Tree. The tree looked weak and sad. Its crystal was missing. Lily looked around carefully. Then she smiled. "The crystal I found belongs to the tree!" She gently placed the crystal back on the tree. Suddenly, the tree began to glow. Bright lights filled the forest. The flowers opened. The birds sang again. Crystal was very happy. "You saved the Magic Tree," she said. As a reward, Crystal gave Lily a small shining crystal. "Keep this crystal," said the fairy. "It will remind you that kindness can make a difference." Lily thanked Crystal and returned home. Every time she looked at the crystal, she remembered her magical adventure. The End.
