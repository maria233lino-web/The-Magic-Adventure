📖 While Reading

Mia was a young girl.

One day, she found a magic backpack in her attic.

The backpack looked old, but it was very special.

Every morning, Mia opened the backpack.

Inside, she found something useful.

On Monday, she found a pencil.

On Tuesday, she found a book.

On Wednesday, she found an umbrella.

Mia used these items to help her friends at school.

Her friends were happy and thankful.

One day, the backpack was empty.

Mia felt sad.

Then she found a note inside.

The note said:

"The real magic is helping others."

Mia smiled.

She understood the lesson.

From that day, she continued helping people every day.

The End.
