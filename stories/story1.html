📖 While Reading

Once upon a time, there was a little puppy named Max. Max lived in a small house near a beautiful park. Every day, he played with his family and ran in the garden. One sunny morning, Max saw a colorful butterfly. He wanted to follow it. The butterfly flew over some flowers and into the park. Max followed the butterfly. He walked and walked until he could not see his house anymore. Suddenly, Max stopped. He looked around. The park was very big. His house was gone. Max felt worried. He barked loudly, but nobody came. After a few minutes, a kind girl named Emma saw Max sitting under a tree. "Are you lost?" she asked. Max wagged his tail. Emma looked at Max's collar and saw his address. She took Max back home. When Max arrived, his family was very happy. They hugged him and thanked Emma for her help. Max learned an important lesson that day: It is good to explore, but it is important to stay close to home. The End.
