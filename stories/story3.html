📖 While Reading

Far away, on a mountain, lived a little dragon named Ember. Every night, Ember saw a strange blue light in the forest. The light moved between the trees and then disappeared. The rabbits were scared. The birds stopped singing. Some animals thought the light was a monster. No one knew what the light was. Ember was curious. One morning, he decided to follow the light. He walked into the forest and found small glowing footprints. The footprints led him to a cave. Inside the cave, Ember saw the blue light again. Then he found a small creature with silver fur and blue eyes. The creature looked sad. "My name is Lumi," she said. "I am lost." Lumi explained that she came from the Crystal Valley. A strong storm had carried her far from home. The blue light was not dangerous. It was Lumi's magic glow. Ember smiled. "I will help you," he said. Together they traveled across hills and rivers. At last, they reached the Crystal Valley. Lumi's family was very happy to see her again. Before Ember left, Lumi gave him a small crystal. "Now you will always remember our friendship," she said. Ember returned to his mountain. Every night, the crystal glowed beside his window. The mysterious blue light was no longer a mystery. It was the beginning of a wonderful friendship. The End.
